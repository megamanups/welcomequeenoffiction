Waste Segregation

A quick overview on the problem

According to The World Bank, the world generates about 2 billion tonnes of solid waste in municipalities every year, yet 33% of that waste isn't managed in a safe manner. In 2021, the Philippines was noted to have contributed to 36.38% of plastic waste in the ocean, being the greatest shareholder. Junior highschool students, despite having waste segregation as a practice, still do not always segregate waste properly.
